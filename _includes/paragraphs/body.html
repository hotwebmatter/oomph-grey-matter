Making strides in systems of care

Advancing collaboration between providers is key to our strategy for making healthcare more affordable. Our systems of care approach—where primary care providers (PCPs) coordinate their patients’ care with specialists and hospitals—has been proven to improve quality and help control costs. The next step forward was to change the way providers are paid. Instead of being reimbursed for each service, 100% of providers in our systems of care are now paid based on the health outcomes for their patients. They can receive higher reimbursement if patients are healthier, and they share in the monetary risk associated with poor health results and cost overruns. To help providers give the best care possible, we share data to help them identify gaps in care, such as members who haven’t received important tests and screenings.
